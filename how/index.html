Our History

We are a very successful company selling online since 2007.

We've started up via bootstrapping, by our own, with a technical and a business co-founder and a budget of a few thousands. Our company operates in a small country of 20 million people and we sell premium gadgets and gizmos as gifts.

After becoming market leader in 2012 we've got an investment from local angel investors building a portfolio of innovative e-commerce companies.

Together we are to double our revenue each year, by investing only 15% in marketing and sales.
This is our story how to become a million dollar business — which we share with you.
